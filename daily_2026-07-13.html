Is Your NPU Ready for LLMs? Dissecting the Hidden Efficiency Bottlenecks in Mobile LLM Inference

这篇论文像「手机跑大模型的体检报告」——把 5 个主流框架 (llama.cpp / GENIE 等) 和 3 种芯片后端 (CPU / GPU / NPU) 都拉出来做对照, 还顺手发明了 PowerBench 工具, 能精确到「每次解码预填到底耗了多少电」。结果发现: NPU 在「预填」（把开头整段长文本塞进去）时跑得贼快, 但在「逐字生成」（一个字一个字往后说）时反而被 CPU 吊打——根本原因是 NPU 喜欢「大块固定形状」的运算, 而解码每次只算一点点, 拼不起来。

📖 专业版问题讲解 (展开)
We present the first comprehensive, cross-layer measurement study of mobile LLM inference, uniquely spanning five mainstream frameworks (e.g., llama.cpp, GENIE) and three hardware backends (CPU, GPU, NPU). We develop PowerBench, a fine-grained profiling tool providing the first backend-specific energy attribution. Three critical insights: (1) framework-induced performance gaps are substantially amplified on NPUs, reaching up to 10× using custom operators due to divergent offloading and quantization strategies; (2) we identify a distinct phase split where NPUs excel at compute-bound prefilling, while CPUs outperform all other backends in memory-bound decoding, driven by the NPU preference for large fixed-shape workloads conflicting with the small-kernel dynamic nature of decoding; (3) backend-specific profiling uncovers substantial scheduling headroom missed by prior device-level measurements.
⚙️ 方法：PowerBench profile tool + cross-framework (llama.cpp/GENIE/etc.) × cross-backend (CPU/GPU/NPU) measurement × 7 models × 5 frameworks × 3 backends, with backend-specific energy attribution. Decoupled prefill vs decode paths, energy decomposition, scheduling headroom analysis.
⚖️ 取舍：Profiling only mobile backends (CPU/GPU/NPU) excludes cloud accelerators (TPU/NVIDIA H100). PowerBench requires kernel-level hooks not available on all commercial NPUs. The phase split insight assumes fixed-shape decode behaviour that may shift with speculative decoding adoption.
📊 效果：(1) Framework-induced gaps amplified up to 10× on NPUs vs custom operators. (2) NPUs excel at compute-bound prefilling, CPUs dominate memory-bound decoding. (3) Backend-aware scheduling yields significant headroom over device-level static allocation. Cross-layer insight: hardware-software coupling must move beyond device-level benchmarking.
🏢 团队：Guanyu Cai, Ruiming Tian, Lang Yang, Zhouhong Ren, Jinliang Yuan, Lingkun Li, Jiliang Wang → Tsinghua University + Beijing Jiaotong University

CTA-Pipelining: A Latency-Oriented Spatial Scaling Method for Multi-GPU Systems

这篇论文提出了叫「CTA 流水线」的并行招式, 专治「多 GPU 共享内存, 但软件还把 GPU 当远端节点用」的低效毛病。具体来说：把 GPU 间的依赖关系拆到 Cooperative Thread Array 这个最细的并行单元上, 让不同的 GPU 在同一个时刻跑不同「接力棒」的 kernel, 而不是等前一颗 GPU 跑完下一颗才开工。实验用的是 8 卡 H200 和 B200, 在 2 层 GEMM（模拟 MLP 操作）上延迟比 micro-batching 降 31.8%, 比 Tensor Parallelism 降 29.6%。

📖 专业版问题讲解 (展开)
We introduce CTA-pipelining, an execution paradigm designed to exploit shared-memory multi-GPU systems. As a latency-oriented spatial scaling technique, CTA-pipelining leverages dependencies at the Cooperative Thread Array level, enabling concurrent execution of dependent kernels across GPUs. We demonstrate its capability using CUTLASS, cuBLAS, and NCCL libraries on 8-GPU H200 and B200 systems. Results show on 2-layer GEMM (representing the MLP operation) CTA-pipelining reduces latency by up to 31.8% compared to micro-batching, and 29.6% compared to TP. It can also be combined with TP as an orthogonal scaling dimension to further push the latency boundary.
⚙️ 方法：CTA-level dependency tracking + CUTLASS/cuBLAS/NCCL kernel modifications + shared-memory multi-GPU scheduling. Evaluated on 8-GPU NVIDIA H200 and B200 nodes. Comparison baselines: micro-batching, Tensor Parallelism (TP).
⚖️ 取舍：Requires shared-memory coherent interconnect (NVLink/NVSwitch), not applicable to cross-node multi-GPU. CTA-level dependency tracking adds implementation complexity vs standard NVSHMEM. Demo limited to 2-layer MLP GEMM; benefit magnitude on larger transformer blocks (8-16 layers) needs further study. Only evaluated on NVIDIA stack—portability to AMD/Intel shared-memory platforms unclear.
📊 效果：31.8% latency reduction vs micro-batching on 2-layer MLP-GEMM. 29.6% reduction vs Tensor Parallelism. Composable with TP for further gains. Validates that shared-memory coherent interconnects (NVLink/NVSwitch) can be exploited at finer granularity than typical NVSHMEM-style distributed programming patterns.
🏢 团队：Tingkai Liu, Muralidhar Andoorveedu, Sanjoy Das, Sanjay Patel, Volodymyr Kindratenko → University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign + NVIDIA Corporation

GPU-Accelerated Effective Resistance Analysis for 3D IC Power Delivery Network

这篇浙江大学的论文把 3D 集成电路 (3D IC) 的电源分配网络 (PDN) 设计的「电阻分析」搬上了 GPU。原本用 CPU 直接求解, 又慢又吃内存; 现在用 GPU 加速, 速度比传统方法提升 5 到 6 个数量级, 而且最大值误差和平均值误差都在可接受范围内。3D IC 是当前 Chiplet / HBM / 先进封装的关键路径, PDN 设计不过关就爆 IR Drop。

📖 专业版问题讲解 (展开)
We propose a GPU-accelerated framework for accurate effective resistance analysis on early-stage 3D IC Power Delivery Networks (PDNs). The proposed framework achieves a speedup of 5 to 6 orders of magnitude compared to the conventional direct solver, while maintaining negligible deviations in both maximum and average relative errors. The method addresses the challenge of TSV (through-silicon via) planning for reliable PDNs, where ill-planned TSVs cause unevenly distributed effective resistance and consequently severe IR drop.
⚙️ 方法：GPU-parallel sparse linear solver + effective resistance decomposition on 3D IC mesh network. Validated against direct CPU solver baseline. Tested on multiple 3D IC benchmark designs with varying TSV counts and stack configurations.
⚖️ 取舍：Effective resistance is one of several PDN metrics (IR drop, electromigration, thermal)—paper does not address multi-objective optimization. GPU memory consumption for very large 3D stacks (16+ tiers) needs verification. Industrial EDA tool integration path not specified (commercial tools use different solver paradigms).
📊 效果：5 to 6 orders of magnitude speedup over conventional direct solver. Negligible deviation in both maximum and average relative error. Enables early-stage TSV planning for PDNs that was previously intractable due to runtime constraints.

Sangam: Efficiently Serving Diffusion LLMs with the AR Stack

这篇论文 (UT Austin + Cisco Research) 解决的是「Diffusion 大语言模型（dLLM）怎么在生产环境跑」。dLLM 不是传统一句一句往外蹦, 而是「一段同时蹦好几个字」, 所以 KV cache 的老办法（每次只算一个字并把之前的缓存起来）玩不转。作者发明了 Sangam 系统, 用一个「deficit token-budget scheduler」, 让正在生成的部分优先跑, 等凑够整块预算才塞进 prefilling, 还能把剩下的预算存到下一轮用。实验证明跨 PD（prefill/decode）分离场景下也能避免饿死。

📖 专业版问题讲解 (展开)
Diffusion language models (dLLMs) generate text by iteratively denoising a masked response and can commit multiple output positions per model invocation. Their bidirectional attention prevents exact autoregressive-style KV caching, since committing one position shifts the KV activations of all others. We present Sangam, a serving system for cached dLLM inference. Sangam introduces a deficit token-budget scheduler that admits in-flight decodes first, admits whole indivisible prefills only when the accumulated token budget allows, and carries unused budget forward to achieve amortized stall-free scheduling. Disaggregated serving avoids prefill-decode interference but suffers from prefill/decode resource partitioning problem; Sangam adopts hybrid serving strategy, overflowing prefills onto decode workers to relieve prefill under-provisioning, and uses the same deficit-budget scheduler to protect those workers' decodes from the overflow.
⚙️ 方法：Deficit token-budget scheduler (admit in-flight decodes first, carry unused budget forward) + disaggregated prefill/decode serving + hybrid overflow strategy (route excess prefills to decode workers) using the same scheduler for decode protection. Implemented on cached dLLM inference engine.
⚖️ 取舍：Specifically designed for cached dLLM inference—transfer to non-cached dLLM serving is not addressed. Hybrid overflow introduces decode-side prefill interference that the paper admits requires further mitigation. Deficit-budget scheduler assumes workload can be characterized a priori; highly bursty traffic may need different admission control.
📊 效果：Achieves amortized stall-free scheduling under varying load. Hybrid strategy overcomes prefill/decode resource partitioning (a key bottleneck in pure disaggregated serving). Validates that AR-style serving mechanisms can be adapted to dLLMs through deficit budgeting rather than exact autoregressive compatibility.
🏢 团队：Nitin Kedia, Saurabh Agarwal, Myungjin Lee, Aditya Akella → The University of Texas at Austin + Cisco Research

Communication-Aware Placement and Pruning for Efficient Mixture-of-Experts Inference

这篇中山大学的工作解决「MoE 大模型推理时, 怎么排专家 + 删专家才不卡」。MoE 模型动辄几百个专家, 每次 forward 要把 token 路由到不同专家, GPU 之间就会因为专家分散「跑断腿」。CAP 框架做了三件事：(1) 把经常被同时激活的专家放一块儿, 减少跨 GPU / 跨节点通信; (2) 给出不同「算力 vs 通信」权衡点, 让用户挑; (3) 选不重要的专家删掉一些 routing 路径, 速度基本不掉。实验比 DeepSeek EPLB 和 vLLM 默认 placement 都快, 单节点和多节点都提升 1.23× 到 1.86×。

📖 专业版问题讲解 (展开)
We propose CAP (Communication-Aware Assignment and Pruning), a framework that considers computation, communication and accuracy together for efficient MoE inference through expert placement and pruning. Three components: (1) Co-activation driven expert placement groups frequently co-activated experts to reduce inter-device and inter-node communication; (2) Communication-computation trade-off adjustment generates placements with different computational load and communication volume; (3) Communication-aware expert pruning selectively removes routing destinations to reduce communication with limited accuracy degradation. Across single-node and multi-node experiments, CAP achieves 1.23×–1.86× throughput improvement over DeepSeek EPLB and sequential placement in vLLM, and preserves better model accuracy at the same target speedup under lossy scenarios.
⚙️ 方法：Co-activation mining from routing traces + communication-aware placement optimization + selective expert pruning with controlled accuracy loss. Implemented as a placement/pruning layer integrated with vLLM and DeepSeek EPLB. Evaluated on single-node and multi-node MoE inference across multiple model scales.
⚖️ 取舍：Co-activation mining requires offline routing trace collection; placement cannot adapt online to distribution shifts. Pruning introduces accuracy cost that may be unacceptable for high-stakes inference (medical/legal). Comparison baselines limited to DeepSeek EPLB and vLLM; not yet tested against Megatron-Core or other production MoE schedulers.
📊 效果：1.23×–1.86× throughput improvement over DeepSeek EPLB and sequential placement in vLLM across single-node and multi-node settings. Better accuracy preservation at equivalent speedup under lossy pruning. Demonstrates that expert placement + pruning can be co-optimized rather than treated separately.
🏢 团队：Xiao Shi, Yingying Sun, Jiangsu Du, Zhiguang Chen, Yutong Lu → Sun Yat-sen University, Guangzhou
